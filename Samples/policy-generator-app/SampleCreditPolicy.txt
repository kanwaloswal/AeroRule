Lending Policy 2026

Restricted Industries:
Customers operating in the 'Gambling' or 'Cannabis' industries are strictly prohibited from receiving loans. Any deal associated with these industries must be declined automatically.

Commitment vs Revenue Threshold:
To ensure sustainable lending, the total commitment of any single deal must not exceed 50% of the customer's total revenue. If the total commitment is greater than 50% of the customer's total revenue, the deal must be flagged for manual review.

Risk Score Minimum:
Customers must have a minimum risk score of 500. If the risk score is below 500, the deal must be declined.
